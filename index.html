About Me

I am a dedicated PhD researcher in Plasma Physics with a strong background in Civil and Infrastructure Engineering with specialization in Environmental Engineering. My research interests lie at the intersection of Plasma Physics, Environmental Engineering, with a particular focus on developing ion mobility spectrometer for real-world environmental applications.

Currently pursuing my PhD at a Comenius university in Bratislava, I have been involved in several research projects that apply plasma physics to solve air quality realted problems in indoor environment.

I am passionate about open-source research and regularly contribute to the scientific community through publications, code sharing, and academic collaborations. My goal is to advance the field of plasma physics.
